💰 Travel Costs Overview

Planning your trip budget is crucial for a smooth and enjoyable experience. Below you'll find estimated costs for accommodation, food, transportation, and essentials commonly needed during travels.

🏨 Lodging Costs

Type Average Cost (Per Night) Description
Budget Hostel $15 - $30 Shared rooms, basic facilities, great for backpackers.
Mid-Range Hotel $50 - $100 Comfortable rooms with amenities like Wi-Fi, breakfast.
Luxury Hotel $150+ High-end services, pools, spas, and premium rooms.
Vacation Rentals $70 - $200 Entire apartments or homes, suitable for groups/families.
